Teacher can make a quiz by adding questions (typing the question and
maybe charts and figures and the 4 choices and specifying the correct
answer) and then submitting and setting a passkey for the quiz. then
he can start the quiz at any time.
Students can take the quiz by entering the passkey and their name.
then they will be able to answer the question until the quiz is closed
by the teacher. After the time for quiz finished, then they will see
their score which is set in the system
then difficulty of questions can be analyzed based on average time
students have spent on them and how many of them have given a correct
answer
